Ahsoka's Lightsabers

Description:

Immerse yourself in the legacy of the Togruta warrior with our meticulously crafted lightsaber set inspired by Ahsoka Tano. This exquisite collection features a matched pair of hilts, including a regal standard-sized lightsaber and a complementary shoto, each designed to reflect the dual-wielding style of the legendary force-wielder.

Details:

Main Lightsaber:

Length: Standard full-size for a commanding presence.

Design: A striking silver and black finish with precise angular cutouts near the emitter for a distinctive silhouette.

Grip: Ergonomically balanced for optimal handling during display or cosplay.

Shoto Lightsaber:

Length: Shorter than the main, for swift and agile off-hand maneuvers.

Design: Mirroring the main lightsaber with a consistent color scheme and style for harmonious symmetry.

Grip: Designed for comfort and control, allowing for a firm grip and fluid movements.
