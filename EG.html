Endgame Gauntlet

Description:

The Endgame Gauntlet is an awe-inspiring collectible that captures the essence of cosmic power. This meticulously crafted piece is a tribute to the legendary artifact capable of bending the very fabric of reality. With its rich burgundy and gold palette, the gauntlet is adorned with six vibrant gem replicas, each representing a different facet of existence. This gauntlet is a striking centerpiece for any collection, radiating the might and majesty of the universe's most formidable power.

Details:
Material Composition: High-quality, durable resin with a metallic finish for a realistic, heavy-metal look without the cumbersome weight.
Color Scheme: An authentic distressed silver base with deep blue accents and weathered battle marks for an experienced warrior's look.
Color Scheme: Deep burgundy and gold, with each gem-colored in hues of purple, blue, red, yellow, green, and orange, creating a vivid display of intergalactic might.
Design Features:
Articulation: Finger joints are articulated for poseable action, allowing for dynamic display options.
Light Features: Each gem features its own LED light, which can be activated with an internal switch for a luminous, otherworldly glow.
Size: Life-sized design, providing an imposing and impressive display or wearable accessory.
Interior Padding: Lined with soft material for comfortable wear during events or while on display.
Dimensions: Fits most adult hands comfortably.
